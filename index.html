About Me

My name is Glory Emmanuel Aviña and I work at Sandia National Laboratories in Livermore, California. I enjoy applying the scientific method to national security problems. My research focuses on cognitive science, enhancing human performance, and cyber security. I've worked at Sandia for 13 years.

I live in Brentwood, CA (~20miles North of Livermore) with my husband (Isaac), one year old little girl (Ariella), and our french poodle ("Ladie" short for Gladiator...long story). We enjoy being outside in the sun (especially on a crisp, autumn day) throwing the frisbee around, and trying new things.
